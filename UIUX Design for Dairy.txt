## 1\. Design Philosophy & Visual Identity

**Concept:** *Digital Pasture.* The interface should feel clean like a laboratory but warm like a farm.

### ðŸŽ¨ Color Palette (Tailwind Config Strategy)

We will move away from "default bootstrap blue." We need colors that look good on screens under the harsh Pakistani sun (high contrast) and in low-light barns (dark mode).

  * **Primary (Brand):** `Emerald-600` (The lush field). Trustworthy, growth-oriented.
  * **Secondary (Action):** `Amber-500` (The sun/corn). Used for primary CTAs and alerts.
  * **Neutral (Surface):** `Stone-50` (Milk/Cream). Avoid pure `#FFFFFF` white; it causes eye strain. Use warm off-whites.
  * **Semantic Colors:**
      * *Success:* `Teal-500` (Vaccination done, Milk logged).
      * *Destructive:* `Rose-600` (Disease alert, Dead).
      * *Warning:* `Orange-400` (Heat detected, Low yield).

### ðŸ”  Typography (Bilingual Mastery)

Since you are using `next-intl`, typography is the backbone of the UI.

  * **English:** **Geist Sans** (Vercel's new font) or **Inter**. Crisp, highly legible numbers (tabular nums are crucial for financial data).
  * **Urdu:** **Noto Nastaliq Urdu** (Google Fonts) or **Gulzar**.
      * *UX Rule:* Ensure `line-height` (leading) is 1.6x for Urdu text containers to prevent character clash.
      * *RTL Support:* All components must support `dir="rtl"` with logical properties (e.g., `ms-2` instead of `ml-2`).

## 3\. Component Design & Aesthetics (Shadcn/ui + Tailwind)

### A. The "Glass-Morphic" Cards (Modern 2025)

Don't use flat cards. Use "Soft depth."

  * **Style:** White background with 70% opacity, `backdrop-blur-md`, subtle `border-stone-200`, and a deep, soft shadow (`shadow-xl shadow-stone-200/50`).
  * **Rounding:** `rounded-2xl` or `rounded-3xl`. The 2025 trend is softer, friendlier shapes.

### B. The Animal Profile (The Core Atom)

This is the most viewed screen. It needs to be beautiful.

  * **Header:** Large photo of the animal (use a placeholder cow avatar if missing).
  * **Badges:** "Pregnant", "Lactating", "Sick" tags using `Badge` component with subtle pastel backgrounds (`bg-green-100 text-green-700`).
  * **Timeline:** A vertical line showing life events (Birth -\> Vaccination -\> Insemination -\> Calving).
  * **Milk Chart:** A clean area chart (Recharts) showing yield trends over the last 30 days.

### C. Data Entry (Frictionless Forms)

Farmers hate typing.

  * **Smart Inputs:** Use sliders for Milk Quantity (0-30 Liters) instead of just text boxes.
  * **Voice Input:** Add a microphone icon to text areas (Notes, Treatments). "Cow 105 has a fever." -\> AI converts to text.
  * **Offline Mode:** If the internet cuts out, show a subtle amber banner: *"Offline mode active. Changes saved locally."* (Sync automatically when back online).

## 5\. Animations & Micro-interactions (Framer Motion)

  * **Page Transitions:** Smooth fade-in and slide-up for content (`y: 20 -> y: 0`).
  * **Button Press:** Scale down (`scale: 0.95`) on click to give tactile feedback.
  * **Loading States:** **Shimmer Effects** (Skeleton loaders) that match the card shape. Do not use generic spinners.

## 6\. Implementation Guide (For the Developer)
